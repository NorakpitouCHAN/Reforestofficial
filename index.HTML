Deforestation

Deforestation is the purposeful clearing of trees and landscapes that can cause catastrophic events.

Effects of deforestation:

.Biodiversity loss: Each year, animals such as birds, koalas, etc. are more likely to lose their natural habitat due to deforestation.

.Floods: Deforestation leads to land erosion because the trees maintain the surface of the mountains. The water level of the rivers increases suddenly, causing floods.
